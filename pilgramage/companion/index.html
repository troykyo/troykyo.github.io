Via Francigena · Lucca to Rome

The Pilgrim Companion

A daily breviary for Troy & Aurelio

Eight days, eight webpages — each a morning office, a reading of the road, a meditation on Scripture, and an evening reflection, drawn from Augustine, Benedict, and the history of the way itself. The arc runs from calling to mission: to reach Rome is to begin a renewed way of living, not to end a journey.

Quotations are reproduced from named editions; historical claims are cited; unverifiable logistics (opening hours, Mass times, stamp points) are linked to official sources rather than stated. Weather is fetched live on each page.
