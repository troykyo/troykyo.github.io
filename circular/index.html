Product design is complex, but designing for lifetimes of products is even more so. Four years of research into Ultra-Personalized Product Service Systems (UPPSS) revealed important challenges faced in designing for Circular Lifetimes. Circular lifetime design required tools and techniques to face the challenge of designing not only the product but also the data flow and customer journey.

As part of the ArcInTexETN research at TU/e Industrial Design, The UPPSS game was created to help designers create Ultra-Personalized Product Service Systems and help develop circular lifetimes. A series of papers were written about UPPSS and the game itself including From Personal to Ultra Personalized
