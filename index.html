Troy is a designer conducting "Researcher through Design" in computational craftsmanship. Troy comes from a practice of fashion design concentrating on knitwear and shoes. His work focuses on technology and techniques needed for the personalization of clothing and accessories using data, along with the software that drives it. Troy investigates on how designers work with data to analyze, design, manufacture, and use clothing tailored to the wearer.
