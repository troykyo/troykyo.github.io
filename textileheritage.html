Textile Heritage Collection

2003

Fashion Institute of Technology

Creativity & Aesthetics, Wearer & Society

This project represented a look into how World Heritage can be reflected in to modern garments for the modern women. Detailed research was performed and many craft techniques were employed to create a series of garments that were historic yet modern at the same time.

All garments were realized by myself and an annotated portfolio accompanied the presentation on the runway.
